Mood signals are generated by taking the current dataset that shows the current emotion status of a living creature.
The current dataset in a mood is generated by predicting the signals that is being amplified through nerves.
Every mood create a different form of feeling known as emotions.
Every emotion exhibit different mood states.
The mood state is a state that is created by the mood signals.

The stimuli is passed through the nerves by means of senses, where the senses create a feeling termed as reaction,
where the reaction is formed depending upon the situation of any living species. 
The result of the reaction creates a mood, thereby forming emotions.
The emotions can be exhibited in the form of silence, happiness, love, sadness, anger and vice versa.
